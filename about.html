Read a little bit about me!

When I was a kid, like every 5-year-old boy at the time, I too was obsessed with legos. I used to collect sets upon sets and I would never stop building. Whether it was creating a rocket ship, a plane, or a robot, I realized that my true passion lied not with playing with the things I built, although that was fun too, but with taking all these small pieces and putting them together. Nowadays as a high-schooler, I still share that same passion for piecing together small things. Only now it's with the goal of building cool stuff that solves problems I see around me!
